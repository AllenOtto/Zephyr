Home Services

Your heating and cooling system works hard all year long to keep your house comfortable. With regular maintenance and service, you can extend the life of your HVAC equipment and keep it operating at peak efficiency. Service includes simple, regular maintenance checkups that are required by most manufacturers for warranties to remain in effect.

When you schedule air conditioning and heating maintenance with The Home Depot, one of our local, licensed, and insured service providers will come to your house and perform a 28-point inspection on your units. Scheduling two seasonal heating and cooling services per year with The Home Depot is a worthwhile investment that can help identify and fix small issues before they lead to bigger, more expensive repairs.

The Home Depot offers maintenance and service for all leading brands of central heating and cooling equipment. You can trust us to perform your HVAC maintenance the right way and stand behind our work.
