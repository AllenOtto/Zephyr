Commercial AC Repair – Air Conditioning Services

Commercial air conditioning repair requires a specific skillset and extensive experience to ensure top-quality results. At Air Depot, our air conditioning repair technicians can quickly assess issues with your HVAC system and provide you with the most effective solutions to restore comfort inside your commercial buildings. This can help you make a good impression on tenants and can increase productivity among employees who work in these facilities by reducing downtime and keeping everyone cool and comfortable in the summer months.

Fast Repairs for Most Common Problems

Our AC repair team can provide rapid response times and quick solutions for the most common HVAC issues in our area, including the following:

  • Blown Fuses
  • Run and start capacitor replacements
  • Thermostat issues
  • Coolant leaks
  • Clogged drain lines
  • Overheated compressors
  • Clogged or dirty condenser and evaporator coils
  • Filter replacements

A Full Lineup of AC Services

The tech team at Unicool also offers commercial maintenance services for your HVAC systems. We perform a comprehensive check on all your AC services and equipment to ensure optimal performance and to allow you to enjoy reduced downtime and improved comfort inside your commercial facilities. Our maintenance agreements include three visits each year to check on the performance of your thermostats, cooling units, humidity control systems and other vital components of your HVAC system. Unicool can also provide specialized cooling services for computer rooms/servers rooms/Data centers and other areas in which temperature control is vital to your continued operation
